Which Light is Which?

The lights for this game will help keep track of who wins the game.

Each column of lights correspond to either the nought or cross player.

Lights 1, 2, and 3 act as counters for X.

Lights 4, 5, and 6 act as counters for O.

After all lights in a column are lit, the game is over and the corresponding player of the symbol wins.
